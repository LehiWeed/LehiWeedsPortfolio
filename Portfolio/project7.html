Reflection

Lessons learned

This project made me consider the design behind everyday inventions—objects we often take for granted but that were carefully designed.

What I like about this artifact

"If there is a design, there is a designer" — the watchmaker analogy is one of my favorite concepts. Participating in this project made me reflect on how practical and effective good design can be.

What I would change

I would have liked a stronger presentation; although it was a group project, I ended up doing most of the work. I would welcome more assignments like this that allow me to apply computational thinking.

How this will help me in the future

In my current career we develop and design many products for clients; this project helped me improve those skills.
